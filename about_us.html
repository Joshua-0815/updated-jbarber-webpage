Jerald's Story
Once in Manila, young boy Jerald loved going to classic barber shops with his father. Nick's Barbershop left a lasting impression on him. It was more than just a place for a haircut; it was a bonding experience between father and son. As Jerald grew older and became a father himself, he wanted to recreate that special barber shop experience. Unable to find what he was looking for, Jerald decided to open his own shop in 1999.


J's Barbershop was born with the vision of providing a first-class experience at an affordable price, reminiscent of Jerald's cherished childhood memories. Word quickly spread, and J's Barbershop became a place where generations could come together, creating new memories while honoring the old. With community support, J's Barbershop expanded to multiple states, fulfilling Ethan's dream and offering solace and relaxation in its unique ambiance. Although Jerald's father passed away in 2003, his memory lives on in J's Barbershop, where every visit is meant to be a memorable experience, strengthening the bond between family members and creating cherished memories.


Today, J's Barbershop stands as a testament to the power of shared experiences, where haircuts and shaves go beyond grooming rituals, becoming moments of connection and love.
